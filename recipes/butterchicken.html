Butter Chicken

Description

This butter chicken recipe is delicious and easy to make in the instant pot. I highly recommend!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Minced ginger
  • Cayenne
  • Garam masala
  • Boneless chicken thighs
  1. Put instant pot to sautee function. Add chicken and spices. Cook until browned
  2. Close instant pot and set pressure cook to high, 10 mins
  3. Open pot, remove chicken and set aside. Blend ingredients to form sauce
  4. Shred chicken with forks, add back to sauce
  5. Serve over rice and enjoy!
