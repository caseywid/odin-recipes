Shrimp Scampi

Description

This shrimp scampi recipe is absolutely delicious. I highly recommend!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Shrimp
  • Linguine
  1. Add butter and oil to pan. Throw in garlic and simmer for a minute. Add wine.
  2. Add shrimp and sautee until cooked
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Put pasta into sauce with the shrimp
  5. Enjoy!
